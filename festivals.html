Festival Traditions

The beautiful rituals and customs that make each festival special for our family.

On Dashain: We apply tika from our elders, fly kites, play cards, and enjoy the most delicious feasts. The whole house comes alive with laughter, music, and the warmth of togetherness.


On Tihar: Each day has a special meaning - from honoring crows and dogs to the beautiful evening of Bhai Tika that celebrates the bond between brothers and sisters.


On Holi: Colors, water balloons, and an abundance of gujiya make this one of the most anticipated days of the year.
